Ant Hunger Wars

This simulation lets you control an army of ants that roam around looking for food.

Blue Ant: Normal Ants looking for food. Red Ant: Rage Ants that can eat other ants. Green Ant: Ants that can eat Rage Ants.

Food comes in two types: normal food and ghost food. Normal food makes ants reproduce, while ghost food makes ants go into rage mode and can eat other ants!

Control the number of ants, the amount of food, and the chance of ghost food. Watch as the ants grow, rage, and battle for survival.

Settings:

  • Number of Ants: The total number of ants in the simulation.
  • Food Count: The number of food items available for ants to find.
  • Ghost Chance: The probability that a food item is a ghost food (0 = no ghost food, 1 = all ghost food).
  • Trail Length: The length of the trail an ant leaves behind.
  • Rage Radius: The distance within which ants enter rage mode when they find ghost food.
  • Food Radius: The distance within which ants can interact with food.
  • Ant Speed: The speed at which ants move in the simulation.
